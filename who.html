Who is Lindsay?

I was born in East London and grew up on the beach, then headed to UCT to study Applied Biology and Marine Biology.

The move from small town to big city changed my life. It fueled my sense of curiosity and drove me towards a career in the fintech startup scene.

I really love the process of learning new things and experiencing something new.

I like to read, eat and spend time outside. A good book teaches me how to view life from another person's perspective. A new recipe teaches me how to keep trying, even when something goes wrong, and how individuals with different strengths come together to create something special. And a new adventure reminds me that there's so much data to collect.

I have so much to learn and I can't wait.
